## About This Collection

Bill Belichick donated his personal football book collection to the United States Naval Academy. The collection contains **568 books** spanning over a century of football history.

### Collection Statistics

- **Football History**: 347 books
- **Biography**: 99 books
- **Instructional**: 61 books
- **Navy/Military**: 18 books
- **General Sports**: 2 books
- **Uncategorized**: 41 books

The oldest books date from the 1890s, including works by Walter Camp, the "Father of American Football." The collection is particularly strong in mid-20th century coaching literature and histories of college football programs.

### Data Sources

This catalogue was compiled from USNA's inventory records. Categories were assigned by USNA staff. The data is still being cleaned and some entries may contain errors or incomplete information.

For difficult-to-find books, try searching by author name alone or consult a research librarian.

### Search Links

Each book has links to four external resources:

- **WC (WorldCat)** - Search global library holdings to find a copy near you
- **IA (Internet Archive)** - May have digitized copies for free online reading
- **AA (Anna's Archive)** - Aggregates multiple sources for digital copies
- **BF (BookFinder)** - Search used book sellers to purchase a copy
